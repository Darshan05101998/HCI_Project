JEE

Engineering entrance examination conducted for admission to various engineering colleges in India.

GMAT

Graduate Management Admission Test is used in admission to many graduate management programs in foreign countries.

CAT

Common Admission Test is a computer based test for admission in a graduate management program in IIM's and many others.

GRE

Graduate Record Examinations Test is used in admission to a graduate engineering/technical programs in foreign countries.

GATE

The GATE exam is a gateway for admission to the various postgraduate engineering programmes offered by the IITs, NIT's etc.

CFA

CFA program is a post-graduate qualification offered by the American-based CFA Institute to financial professionals.
